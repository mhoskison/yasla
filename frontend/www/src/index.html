YASLA

Yet Another Shopping List App

Features

Manage your shopping lists

  • Create multiple shopping lists
  • Manage your products easily and conveniently
  • Collaborate with friends and family

Integrated with Tesco API

  • Search for any product in the Tesco catalogue
  • Full product descriptions and up-to-date pricing
  • View images before you buy!

Choice of platform

  • Web app
  • Android app
  • Electron desktop app

Built with modern technologies

  • PHP7.1 & Laravel5.5
  • AngularJS & AngularJS Material
  • Cordova & Electron

About

YASLA is a portfolio project developed while I'm looking for my next exciting full stack web developer position. Whilst it's functional in what it does, the intention is not to release yet another shopping list app, but to act as a showcase for modern web development technologies.

YASLA is free (as in beer) and open source - the entire codebase for this web site, the web app and the Android app are available on GitHub. Feel free to check it out, fork the code and develop your own masterpiece!
